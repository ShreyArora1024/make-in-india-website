  1. NEW HEALTH AND WELLNESS CENTRES
    • In a bid to make healthcare more accessible, new Health and Wellness centres are being established with USD 185 Mn already allocated. These centres will provide essential drugs and diagnostics services free of cost. The centres are also designed to provide comprehensive health care, including treatment and medication for non-communicable diseases as well as maternal and child health services.
